Problem

Academics have collected lots of information on how people learn but it has not been translated into a practical structured resource for teachers.

Mission

  • To provide a structured framework
  • for supporting different learning styles
  • in an accessible format for teachers.

What is POWS?

Picture, Object, Words and Story

Are the 4 elements that make up a great lesson, if a teacher can use these 4 areas to teach a subject the majority of different learning styles are covered. This will help engage all students and help them learning in a personalised way.

Who is it for?

It is for everyone, as everyone learns in different ways.

There are been lots of academic research done into the different ways people learn, some key differences in learning styles are: visual, verbal, kinasthetic and contextual.

What does it look like?

All the lesson plans are going to be kept in an organised database that teachers can access and edit online.

Lessons can be organised into 1 of the 4 categories (or multiple categories): Pictures, Objects, Words and Stories.

Each lesson will have access to the resources need to teach it, these resources can be easily edited.
