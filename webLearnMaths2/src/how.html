How does POWS work?

The Activities

Each activity is associated with a POWS, a topic and a subject.

The Lessons

Teachers can combine activities together to make lessons.

The Database

All the lessons and activities are stored in a Database that is easily navigable.

The Community

Teachers can log in and add their own lessons and download other peoples lessons.
