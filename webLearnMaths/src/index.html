Abstract

Dicho Maths seeks to make mathematics accessible for all children, using adaptable resource that can be picked up and used by teachers.

The resources are a practical realisation of current Learning Theories and Learning Styles.

Dicho Maths will focus on the interplay between quantity, visual, and verbal representations of numbers using physical and digital resources.
